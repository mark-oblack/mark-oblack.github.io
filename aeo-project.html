American Eagle Outfitters Client Project

Objective

Create a voice recognition (VR) and/or contextualized app experience to support American Eagle Outfittersâ€™ (AEO) customer and associate needs.

Research

My team distributed an online survey in order to learn common frustrations or pain points AEO customers experience when shopping in store and online. Our target audience consisted of 43 individuals ages 18-40. Based on our findings, my team learned that customers struggle with getting a sense of how clothes fit and feel when shopping online.

Solution

Based on the research findings, my team focused on developing two features that provides customers with information on how an item will fit and feel based on

  • Their own body measurements

  • Any similar clothing items they may have previously purchased.

The resulting ideas, dubbed AEO Closet and AEO True Fit, aim to alleviate these pain points for customers.

My Role

In this project, I was primarily responsible for creating task flows, sketches, and a high-fidelity prototype of the AEO app that integrated our solutions. In addition, I oversaw usability testing of the app and making any modifications based on user feedback.
