AEO Client Project

Goal

Create a Voice Recognition (VR) and/or contextualized app experience to support our customer and associate needs.

Result

Our team conducted user research with surveys to learn more about users' painpoints and struggles with shopping online and in a brick-and-mortar store. Our research showed that many users struggle with figuring out how an item fits and feels when shopping online. Based on this, we focused on developing two features that allow users to learn how an item will fit and feel based on a) their own body measurements and b) any similar clothing items they may have previously purchased. The resulting ideas, dubbed AEO Closet and AEO True Fit, aim to alleviate these painpoints for customers.

We invite you to view our project and presentation files. Thank you for providing us the opportunity to participate in this project and present our findings!
