Monthly Theme

As a way to challenge myself to grow into the field of design, I started to select a monthly theme that I would focus my designs around. Some themes could be generic, like fall or superheroes, while others could be a specific style of art, such as flat art. From there, I would draft up different designs based around that theme. In some cases, the designs could range from posters to logos to desktop wallpapers. During the month, I will try to desing 1-2 pieces per week. With each piece, I try to cover each step of the basic design process. These steps include sketching the design with the ol' reliable pencil and paper, selecting typefaces and a color palette, and then designing the piece using Illustrator.
