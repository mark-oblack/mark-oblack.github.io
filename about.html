About

My name is Mark O'Black and my passion lies in user interface and experience design. Careerwise, my goal is to become a web developer that incorporates UI/UX principles to provide consumers with a pleasant, enjoyable experience when visiting my site. Currently, I am working as a communications specialist for the IT department at Duquesne University.

In addition to my job, I am pursuing a Master's degree in Digital Media Arts with a focus on interface and experience design. When I am not working on assignments for class, I like to spend time learning about front-end web development and graphic design through resources such as Lynda.com, Team Treehouse and freeCodeCamp. In May 2017, I completed a web design and development certificate at Duquesne University. Topics covered in this program included HTML5/CSS3/JSE6, UI/UX design, and database management.

Aside from work and school, I enjoy reading, cheering on the Pittsburgh Penguins and running abnormally long distances (I've completed 2 full marathons and 4 half marathons).

If you would like to learn more or discuss a potential design project, feel free to reach me through one of the methods listed below or on the Contact page. Thanks for visiting!

About

My name is Mark O'Black and I'm currently studying front-end web development and user experience design. My goal is to become a web developer that incorporates UX design principles to provide users with a pleasant, enjoyable experience when visiting a site or app. I currently work as an IT service professional at Duquesne University in Pittsburgh, PA.
